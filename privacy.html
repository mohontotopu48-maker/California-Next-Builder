Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Information We Collect

When you request a service on California Next Builder, we collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, property ZIP code, and the details regarding the service you require. We also automatically collect standard web tracking payload data for analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is primarily used to match you with verifying local home service professionals. By submitting your phone number, you consent to being contacted by our system (via SMS, calls, or email) and by our partnered contractors regarding your project estimate.

3. Sharing of Information

We act as a routing platform. Therefore, the details of your service request are shared exclusively with the contractors within our network who are best positioned to fulfill your request in Orange and LA County.
